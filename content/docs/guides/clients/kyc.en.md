KYC (Know Your Customer) is a mandatory company verification procedure. Without KYC verification, you will not be able to see prices in the catalog or create deals.

## Why Verification Is Needed

- **Access to prices** -- prices in the catalog are hidden until verification is complete
- **Deal creation** -- only verified companies can submit procurement requests
- **Security** -- verification protects all platform participants from fraud

## Required Documents

Prepare scans or photos of the following documents:

| Document | Format | Max size |
|----------|--------|:--------:|
| Registration certificate | PDF, JPG, PNG | 10 MB |
| TIN certificate | PDF, JPG, PNG | 10 MB |
| Company charter (first and last pages) | PDF | 10 MB |
| Power of attorney (if submitted by someone other than the director) | PDF, JPG, PNG | 10 MB |

{{< callout type="info" >}}
Documents must be legible. Blurry or cropped scans will be rejected.
{{< /callout >}}

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1. Open the Verification section

Log in to your account and go to **"Company Settings" > "KYC Verification"**. You will see the current verification status and the document upload form.

### Step 2. Fill in company details

Provide or verify the following:

- Full company name
- Legal address
- Actual address (if different)
- Bank details (settlement account, bank code, bank name)
- CEO full name and position

### Step 3. Upload documents

For each document, click **"Upload file"** and select the corresponding file. After uploading, you will see a preview of the document.

<!-- TODO: replace with a screenshot of KYC document upload -->
![Document upload -- previews of uploaded files](/images/screenshots/placeholder.svg)

### Step 4. Submit for review

Click the **"Submit for review"** button. The status will change to **"Under review"**.

## Verification Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Not submitted** | Documents have not been uploaded yet |
| **Under review** | A manager is reviewing your documents |
| **Revision required** | Documents need to be corrected or supplemented |
| **Verified** | Verification passed, full platform access granted |
| **Rejected** | Verification rejected (see the reason in the comment) |

## Review Timeline

- **Standard timeline:** 1--2 business days
- You will receive a notification via email and in your account about the result

{{< callout type="warning" >}}
If the status is "Revision required", open the verification section, read the manager's comment, upload the corrected documents, and resubmit.
{{< /callout >}}

## After Successful Verification

After passing KYC you gain access to:

1. Prices displayed in the catalog
2. Ability to create deals and requests
3. Excel specification import
4. Receiving commercial quotes

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I speed up the review?

Contact your manager via the platform chat or by phone listed in the contacts section.

### Documents were rejected -- what should I do?

Read the manager's comment in the verification section. The most common reasons are: illegible scan, outdated document, or data mismatch with the information provided during registration.
