To start using the RailTech B2B platform, you need to register your company account. Registration is free and takes no more than 5 minutes.

## Requirements

Before registering, prepare the following:

- **Company TIN** (Taxpayer Identification Number)
- **Corporate email** -- a confirmation email will be sent to this address
- **Contact phone number** -- for your manager to reach you

{{< callout type="info" >}}
The platform is designed for legal entities. Individual registration is not supported.
{{< /callout >}}

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1. Open the registration page

Go to the main page at [railtech.uz](https://railtech.uz) and click the **"Register"** button in the top right corner.

### Step 2. Fill in company details

Complete the registration form:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|:--------:|
| Company name | Full legal entity name | Yes |
| TIN | 9-digit taxpayer identification number | Yes |
| Email | Corporate email address | Yes |
| Phone | Contact number with country code | Yes |
| Password | Minimum 8 characters, letters and digits | Yes |
| Industry | Select from the list | No |

<!-- TODO: replace with a screenshot of the company registration form -->
![Registration form -- fields: name, TIN, email, phone, password](/images/screenshots/placeholder.svg)

### Step 3. Confirm your email

After submitting the form, a confirmation email with a link will be sent to the specified address. Click the link in the email.

{{< callout type="warning" >}}
If the email does not arrive within 5 minutes, check your Spam folder. If the email is still missing, click "Resend" on the registration page.
{{< /callout >}}

### Step 4. Log in

After confirming your email, you can log in to your account using your email and password.

## What Happens After Registration

After successful registration:

1. **A Workspace is created** for your company -- you become its Owner
2. **You are assigned the Client role** -- you can browse the catalog, but **prices are hidden** until KYC is completed
3. **A RailTech manager is notified** about the new registration

{{< callout type="info" >}}
To access prices and create deals, you need to complete [KYC verification](../kyc/).
{{< /callout >}}

## Common Problems

### "TIN already registered"

Your company already has an account on the platform. Contact your company administrator or our support team: **support@railtech.uz**.

### "Invalid TIN"

Check that the TIN is entered correctly. The TIN must contain 9 digits. If the error persists, contact support.
