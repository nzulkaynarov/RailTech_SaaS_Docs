The RailTech B2B catalog contains thousands of railway equipment items, spare parts and industrial goods. This guide explains how to efficiently find the items you need.

## Catalog Structure

The catalog is divided into three domains:

| Domain | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Railway Equipment** | Rolling stock, spare parts, assemblies | Wheelset, bogie, gearbox |
| **General Goods** | Industrial goods and consumables | Bearings, lubricants, tools |
| **Services** | Maintenance and professional services | Diagnostics, repair, certification |

Railway Equipment has a strict category hierarchy linked to equipment groups. General Goods uses a flexible structure with customizable attributes.

## Searching for Products

### Quick Search

Use the search bar at the top of the catalog. Enter a product name, article number or keywords.

**Search tips:**

- Use an exact article number for quick results: `KP-100M`
- Search by partial name: `wheelset`
- The system supports searching by technical specifications

### Filtering by Categories

A category tree is located to the left of the product list. Click on a category to view only its products.

<!-- TODO: replace with a screenshot of the catalog category tree -->
![Catalog -- category tree on the left, product list on the right](/images/screenshots/placeholder.svg)

For Railway Equipment, additional filtering by **equipment group** is available (e.g., "Locomotives", "Wagons", "Track machinery").

### Additional Filters

Depending on the category, the following filters are available:

- **Manufacturer** -- select one or more
- **Country of origin** -- filter by country
- **Availability** -- only items in stock
- **Technical parameters** -- depend on category (diameter, power, weight, etc.)

## Product Card

Click on a product in the list to open its full card.

<!-- TODO: replace with a screenshot of a product card -->
![Product card -- name, photo, specifications, price](/images/screenshots/placeholder.svg)

The card contains:

- **Basic information** -- name, article number, description
- **Technical specifications** -- parameter table
- **Photos** -- product image gallery
- **Category and equipment group** -- position in the catalog hierarchy
- **Price** -- displayed only for verified clients

{{< callout type="warning" >}}
If you see "Price on request" instead of a price, you need to complete [KYC verification](../kyc/).
{{< /callout >}}

## Adding to a Request

From the product card, you can immediately add an item to a request:

1. Specify the **quantity**
2. Click **"Add to request"**
3. Select an existing deal or create a new one

For more details on creating deals, see [Creating a Request](../deals/).

## Units of Measurement

The catalog uses standard units of measurement:

- **pcs** -- pieces (for discrete items)
- **sets** -- complete sets
- **kg**, **t** -- kilograms, tonnes (for weight-based items)
- **m**, **lin. m** -- meters, linear meters
- **l** -- liters (for liquids)
