When the RailTech manager has calculated the cost for your request, you will receive a commercial quote. This guide describes how to work with quotes on the platform.

## How You Receive a Quote

1. The manager processes your request and sources suppliers
2. The cost is calculated including logistics, customs and margin
3. You receive a **notification via email** and **in your account**
4. The deal stage changes to **"Quote Sent"**

## Viewing the Commercial Quote

### Step 1. Open the deal

Go to **"Deals"** and open the deal with the **"Quote Sent"** status.

### Step 2. Go to the Quote tab

On the deal page, open the **"Quote"** or **"Commercial Quote"** tab.

### Step 3. Review the quote

The quote contains a table with a breakdown for each item:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Name | Proposed product |
| Quantity | Requested quantity |
| Unit price | Cost per unit (including delivery) |
| Amount | Subtotal per item |
| VAT (12%) | VAT amount |
| Total with VAT | Final cost |
| Delivery time | Expected delivery timeline |
| Notes | Manager's comment |

At the bottom of the table, the following totals are displayed:

- **Subtotal (excl. VAT)** -- sum across all items
- **VAT 12%** -- total tax amount
- **Total (incl. VAT)** -- final amount due

{{< callout type="info" >}}
All prices are stated in Uzbek soums (UZS). Currency exchange rates are fixed as of the quote generation date.
{{< /callout >}}

## Actions on the Quote

### Approve the Quote

If the quote meets your requirements:

1. Click **"Approve"**
2. Confirm the action in the pop-up window
3. The deal will move to the **"Contract Signed"** stage
4. The manager will contact you to formalize the contract

### Request Changes

If adjustments are needed:

1. Click **"Request changes"**
2. Describe what needs to be changed (price, quantity, item replacement)
3. The manager will be notified and will prepare an updated quote

You can also discuss details via the [deal chat](../chat/).

### Reject the Quote

If the quote does not meet your needs:

1. Click **"Reject"**
2. Specify the reason for rejection
3. The deal will be closed, but you can create a new request later

{{< callout type="warning" >}}
Rejecting a quote is not final. You can always create a new request with updated requirements.
{{< /callout >}}

## Downloading the Quote as PDF

You can download the quote as a PDF for internal approval:

1. Click **"Download PDF"** on the quote page
2. The file will be saved to your computer

The PDF version contains all quote data, party details and a QR code for quick access to the deal on the platform.

## Quote Validity

A commercial quote is valid for **14 days** from the date it was sent (unless the manager specifies a different period). After the validity period expires, prices may be recalculated.
